ExplodingTNT - Age, Family, Bio

 Posted on April 9, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  122 words  |  Enda Jantzen

YouTube phenomenon named Sam whose Minecraft machinimas and trolling videos have helped him reach more than 5 million subscribers. He has been widely known for creating a Minecraft character called Pink Sheep. He launched a secondary YouTube channel dedicated to Pink Sheep that has earned over 1 million subscribers. 

Before Fame

He initially created his YouTube channel to make videos about exploding statues. 

Trivia

He owns minigame servers called McOrigins and The Nova. His "If Lava and Water Switched Places - Minecraft" video from June of 2014 has racked up over 18 million views. 

Family Life

He is from Toronto, Canada and has featured his younger brother on his channel. 

Associated With

Deadlox, SkyDoesMinecraft, and JeromeASF have all been guests on his channel.
