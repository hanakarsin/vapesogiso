Kanayo O Kanayo Reveals Sacrifice He Offers To Make Money

 Posted on June 7, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  189 words  |  Enda Jantzen

Nollywood veteran actor Anayo Modestus Onyekwere, popularly known as Kanayo O Kanayo, has opened up on the sacrifice he offers to make his money.

Due to the role the actor often play in movies, some people have alleged that the actor is involve in blood rituals.

However, the actor has denied being involved in any form of rituals to make money.

In a video posted on his Instagram page, the veteran has opened up on the sacrifice he does to make money.

In the video shared on his Instagram page, Kanayo and his team could be seen awake and working at 1am.

He said while others are asleep, he works hard and make his money, stressing that that is the true sacrifice.
