A Hairstylist Finds Young Girl Who Was Teased About Her Hair, Gives Her a New Sleek Look

 Posted on March 28, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  697 words  |  Lourie Helzer
  • A hairstylist from Chicago, US, delighted netizens after giving a young girl an extreme makeover to boost her self confidence
  • The young lass was being teased after boys in her school took a picture of her hairstyle to mock her
  • According to the hairstylist, she saw the need to track the baby girl and spoilt her with a new haircut, and now the young girl is a brand ambassador for the stylist's business.

A hairstylist from Chicago in the US has warmed the hearts of people online after posting a moving story of how she saved a young girl from trolls she was getting at school by giving her a new hairstyle makeover.

The hair expert posted the moving story on her Twitter page @ShaeStyles_, and write about the young girl's struggle with her hairstyle.
