Precious Chikwendu Speaks On Going Back To Ex-Husband Fani Kayode

 Posted on February 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  289 words  |  Chauncey Koziol

Former Beauty Queen, Precious Chikwendu has reacted to speculations of returning to her ex-husband, Femi Fani-Kayode, barely two years after fighting over custody of their children.

Naija News reports that this comes after Precious and FFK were recently spotted at the house party of Senator Sani Musa.

However, a blog that shared photos from the event alleged that the couple are currently working on their marriage as Fani Kayode wants Precious back.

Reacting to the post, Precious Chikwendu debunked the speculations, stating that she will be bold enough to call a press conference to announce her return to FFK if she wishes to do so.

She further questioned if she was supposed to avoid her ex-husband for public satisfaction and urged critics who feel it is wrong to share space with the former Aviation Minister to produce customised family friends, air and life for her.

She wrote: “Source please! I am not having myself dancing in any speculations this time. I am bold enough to call a press conference to announce my return if i do wish to do a 360. You all like hypocrites. I take it i am also to wash off the memories of everyone i knew as a wife as well as hate their wives and not honor them because my ex husband is at their party and avoid him must i for public satisfaction abi?

“Since i must share kids and not share air and space, produce a customized air, family friends and life for me accordingly.

“Plus this ain’t newsworthy, were it to be true. Lets apply the pressure dedicated to such gist to voting in a competent president that would help most of us be busy enough to ignore baseless speculations.”
