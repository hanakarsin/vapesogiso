Wife Of Juju Singer, King Sunny Ade Is Dead

 Posted on August 11, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  203 words  |  Enda Jantzen

The wife of popular Nigerian Juju singer, Chief Sunday Adeniyi Adegeye, better known as King Sunny Ade, has reportedly died.

A report obtained by Naija News revealed that Hon Risikat Ajoke Adegeye died on Tuesday morning.

The deceased is a member of the ruling All Progressives Congress, and was once the member representing Amuwo- Odofin Local Government at the Lagos State House of Assembly.

She served between 2011 and 2015.

No comment has been made yet by the African pop musician and the cause of Hon. Adegeye’s death remains unknown as of the time of filing this report.
