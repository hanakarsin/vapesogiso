 Posted on January 1, 0001  |  7 minutes  |  1339 words  |  Almeda Bohannan

You cannot talk about gospel music in South Africa without mentioning Joyous Celebration songs. The group is renowned for creating a harmony of music from its group of contralto, soprano and tenor singers, which bless every person that listens to them.

Joyous Celebration is one of the most successful gospel choirs in South Africa. The group was formed in the year 1994 in the aftermath of the first successful democratic elections in the country. Joyous Celebration boasts of more than 30 members. Most of the songs released by the group are in the Zulu language, but there are a few English tracks available.

List of the best Joyous Celebration songs

The much-celebrated South African gospel group has released more than 20 albums to date. Their best songs have garnered more than 1 million views on their YouTube channel. Here are some of the best praise and worship songs by Joyous Celebration that will uplift your spirit.
