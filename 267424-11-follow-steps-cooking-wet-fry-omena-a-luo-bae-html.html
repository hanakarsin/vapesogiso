Ingredients

•2 onions

•3 large ripe tomatoes

•coriander leaves

•Green Bella pepper

•1 Royco cubes (spices of your choice)

•Salt to taste

•2 gloves of garlic grated

Steps

1.Pick clean Omena and get rid of any foreign residues

2.Wash Omena and let it sit for about 3 minutes

3.Make sure the water is clear and if not repeat the cleaning process until it sits on clean water.
