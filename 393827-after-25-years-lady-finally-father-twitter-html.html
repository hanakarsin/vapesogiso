Lady reunites with dad 25 years after he abandoned her mother

- A 25-year-old lady, Keaira, has reunited with her dad decades after he left his family

- The lady sent the man named Jeremiah a message on social media to notify him that she was his daughter

- At first, the man was shocked but he finally told her where he lived

A lady named Keaira is counting her lucky stars after finally tracking down her long lost dad.

The woman and her father had last seen each other 25 years ago before he abandoned her and her mother.

On Father’s Day, she sent a simple message to the man identified as Jeremiah.

She told the man that she found out they were related a few years ago.

Keaira said she did not expect anything from Jeremiah and only wanted to meet him and put a face to his name.

The ballsy woman told her dad she was 25 years old and encouraged him to speak to her anytime he needed a listening ear.

"Hey Jeremiah, my name is Keaira. I learnt a few years back that you may be my biological father so I thought I should reach out," a screenshot of her message read.

Jeremiah was pleasantly surprised and sent the woman his address.

“Omg, I am lost for words right now. I believe I remember your mother and I would love to hear from you. I live in Lithonia," he replied.

The two met and the rest, as they say, is history.

In other news by TUKO.co.ke, a Kenyan woman also got the rare chance to reunite with her family after being apart for many years.

Esther Chebet said she was separated from her family in 2003 when she was still a child after her parents separated.

Her father, who worked in the army, took her together with her siblings from their mother and to their grandfather's home.

When he died, Chebet was taken in by her aunt but she did not know who took her sibling and she never saw them again.

She was reunited with them by Kameme Fm's radio host Man Nyari on Sunday, November 7.
