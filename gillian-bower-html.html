Gillian Bower - Age, Family, Bio

 Posted on April 23, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  124 words  |  Lourie Helzer

Specializing in DIY content, she is a YouTube content creator who also publishes life hack, haul, and decor videos for her 2.2 million subscribers. 

Before Fame

She initially created her YouTube channel in January of 2014 with the username thinkkbeautyy. Her first video is titled "Cute Hairstyles for a Baseball Cap." 

Trivia

In 2016, some of her most watched videos became "10 Fun Things To Do When You're Bored!," "15 Life Hacks You Need to Know!," and "DIY Glitter Sleep Balm EOS! Sleep Easy with NO Drugs!" 

Family Life

In April of 2017 she wished her labrador retriever a happy birthday on Instagram, calling him her best friend. 

Associated With

She once posted a makeup tutorial inspired by Lucy Hale's Pretty Little Liars character Aria Montgomery.
