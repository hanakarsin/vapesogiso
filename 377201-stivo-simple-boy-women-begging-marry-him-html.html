Stivo Simple Boy says women are all over him begging to marry him

- Stivo Simple Boy said in an interview that women were professing love to him and want him to be their man for life

- The musician said finding the right woman was his problem and asked ladies to give him more time

- The Mihadarati hitmaker also mentioned some of the attributes of a lady he wanted to marry which included her being saved

Fast-rising musician Stivo Simple Boy has revealed girls are begging him for his hand in marriage.

The Kibera faithful singer has always insisted he is not looking for love and being a virgin, he wants to wait till marriage to finally enjoy a woman's warmth.

During an interview with TUKO.co.ke's Lynn Ngugi, Stivo said women from all over were now professing love to him and begging for his hand in marriage.

"Women say 'Ah Simple Boy I have feelings for you and want to marry you.' Many of them, but you just have to tell them to wait until God decides then I will choose one to marry," he said.

When asked why he hasn't found Mrs right, Stivo said finding the right woman was the problem and asked ladies to give him more time.

He also gave attributes of the lady he wanted to marry which included her being saved.

"I look at the behaviour, she must be loyal to me, then respectful and saved," he added.
