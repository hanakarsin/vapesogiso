On Saturday, May 8, Deputy President William Ruto's daughter June Ruto's Nigerian fiance', Alex Ezenagu and his family, visited the DP for dowry negotiations in an exquisite ceremony.

June's choice of man was, yet again, a blow to Kenyan men after she joined the growing list of female celebrities and women at large who are giving Kenyan men a wide berth and choosing to instead settle for Nigerian men as life partners.

TUKO.co.ke looks at six Kenyan female public figures who found love in Nigeria.

1. June Ruto

It is now no secret that Ruto's daughter June's lover is from Nigeria. This is after the man, Ezenagu and his family flew all the way to meet the deputy president for dowry negotiations.

A spot check by TUKO.co.ke revealed Ezenagu is an accomplished man who works as an assistant Law Professor at the Hamad Bin Khalifa University.

2. Emmy Kosgey

Award-winning gospel artist Emmy Kosgei and her pastor husband Anselm Madubuko are a great couple and very much in love despite their huge age difference.

Emmy and Anselm, the general overseer of Revival Ministries, have been married for eight years and live in Nigeria.

3. Ruth Matete

In late 2019, Tusker Project Fame (TPF) winner Ruth Matete said 'I do' to her late Nigerian husband Pastor John.

4. Amani

Amani who converted from a secular to a gospel musician is married to her Nigerian soulmate Chinasa Udeala.

The couple tied the knot in a beautiful private ceremony. They welcomed their first-born child a few months ago.

5. Wavinya Ndeti

Long-serving Machakos politician and Transport Chief Administrative Secretary (CAS) Wavinya Ndeti was also married to a Nigerian man Dolamu Henry Oduwole.

Oduwale passed away in 2016.

6. Businesswoman Akinyi

Renowned businesswoman Akinyi Chinedu's marriage to her Nigerian lover Anthony Chinedu culminated in a nasty divorce but nonetheless, she still falls under this list.

The couple formalised the union in 2004 but had, by then brought fourth and were living together. In 2009, they went their separate ways after a messy divorce.
