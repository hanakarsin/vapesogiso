Jett Klyne - Age, Family, Bio

 Posted on March 29, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  81 words  |  Chauncey Koziol

Television and film actor who is widely recognized for his portrayal of Tommy Maximoff on the Disney+ series WandaVision. He has also appeared on Chilling Adventures of Sabrina and The X-Files.

Before Fame

He made his professional acting debut in a 2013 film titled Heart of Dance.

Trivia

In 2016, he guest-starred on an episode of Supernatural. 

Family Life

He is from Penticton, British Columbia. Both of his parents grew up in the British Columbia town of Oliver.

Associated With

He appeared on WandaVision alongside Elizabeth Olsen and Paul Bettany.
