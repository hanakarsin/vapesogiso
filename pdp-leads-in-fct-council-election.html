PDP Leads In FCT Council Election

 Posted on March 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  263 words  |  Chauncey Koziol

The Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) is currently in an early as votes collation resumes at the Local Government centre Sunday.

Recall that the Independent National Electoral Commission, INEC, had on Saturday conducted elections into six Chairmanship seats and 62 Councillorship seats across the six Area Councils of the FCT.

It was gathered that the PDP is currently leading in 10 out of 11 Council Wards collated, with the results from Kabusa Ward yet to be counted.
