Former BBnaija Star Proposes To US-Based Girlfriend

 Posted on July 17, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  236 words  |  Chauncey Koziol

Former BBNaija housemate, Tochukwu Okechukwu, popularly known as Tochi, has proposed to his US-based girlfriend Chioma.

The reality TV star took to his Instagram page to share a video of himself on his knees while he placed an engagement ring on his girlfriend’s finger.

Friends of the couple who graced the event sprayed naira notes on them to celebrate their new status.

Tochi, who is a fast rising songwriter, entertainer and entrepreneur, was the 11th housemate to get into the BBNaija season 5 house.

He is from Imo state and is currently 28-years-old.

In other news, One of Ubi Franklin’s baby mamas and businesswoman,Sandra Iheuwa’s marriage to her husband, Steve Thompson, has crashed.

According to Mr Thompson, his marriage to Sandra can not work as she is  a social media freak, who doesn’t care much about their home.
