On Saturday, the couple was on NTV's Gumzo La Sato show, where they shared their story with hosts Frida Mwaka and Lofty Matambo.

Advising those in a relationship like his, Greg, a German national, said:

"Take what other people say with a pinch of salt. Invest into your relationship."

The two are blessed with twin boys, and speaking about relationships, Greg said:

"Whether white or black, gay or straight, or different religions, it doesn't matter what other people say about your relationship, ultimately, you're only the only people that know. Just give thumbs up to your partner and don't care about everybody else."
